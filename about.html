About Us

Welcome to Ibara Christian Centre, a place where those of all backgrounds can gather together and worship. We are committed to providing an environment that nurtures spiritual growth, encourages connection with the divine but has more than just traditional ministry at its core. Our mission is not only focused on teaching you how to be closer with God through prayer, music and scripture reading; we also encourage discipleship for our members in search to be like Christ.

Our Values.

Discipleship

It’s impossible to overstate the power that church discipleship values can have on our lives. The act of following Jesus and being connected with a faith community is an experience filled with love, compassion, understanding, and accountability – all qualities that are essential in nurturing character growth. As human beings naturally strive to improve ourselves, church provides us with moral reinforcement along the journey.By committing to other believers through weekly gatherings and intentional relationships focused around Christ-centered principles, we find stability when enduring difficult obstacles or trying times.

Teaching

At Ibara Christian Centre, we strive to provide a space where these core principles remain important and are actively shared with adults and children alike on Sundays as well as at home during the week. Ultimately God wants us to use these teachings to live compassionate lives,Any church with it's words will most likely have values that guide how it operates and ensures everyone involved is on the same page when it comes to expectations about things like respect, love, tolerance, and service.
These values are essential for creating a cohesive environment where faith can thrive. At ICC we teach our members to build our relationship with God.
